# Business Model Canvas

## Customer Segments
- Medical device manufacturers (large and small) - $50B+ global market
- Healthcare technology companies developing integrated solutions
- Research institutions and universities creating medical innovations
- Hospitals and healthcare systems with custom device requirements
- Startups entering the medical device space with limited regulatory expertise

## Value Propositions
- Streamlined development platform reducing time-to-market by 40% for medical-grade software
- Built-in compliance frameworks for FDA, CE, and other global medical regulations
- Seamless integration capabilities for hardware, firmware, and cloud components
- Pre-validated security protocols meeting HIPAA, ISO 13485, and IEC 62304 standards
- Automated documentation generation for regulatory submissions (saves ~200+ hours per submission)

## Channels
- Direct sales team specializing in medical technology (USD 15k-30k/month per rep, THB 500k-1M/month)
- Industry conferences and trade shows (HIMSS, MedTech, etc.) - USD 20k-50k/event
- Online marketing through medical technology publications and journals
- Partnerships with regulatory consultants and medical device incubators
- Medical device development communities and forums

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated technical support with medical device expertise (24/7 for enterprise clients)
- Regulatory update subscriptions and compliance guidance
- Community forums for peer-to-peer problem solving
- Account management for larger enterprise clients (USD 5k-15k/month per account)
- Educational webinars and training programs on medical device development

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription licensing: USD 5k-50k/month per organization (THB 165k-1.65M/month)
- Implementation and integration services: USD 10k-100k per project
- Compliance consulting packages: USD 5k-25k per regulatory submission
- Premium support contracts: 20-30% of subscription fee
- Volume-based pricing for larger organizations: Tiered pricing based on development teams

## Key Resources
- Medical device regulatory expertise team (USD 150k-300k/year per expert, THB 5M-10M/year)
- Secure development infrastructure with healthcare compliance certifications
- Compliance documentation templates and regulatory knowledge base
- Integration frameworks for common medical hardware (ECG, imaging, sensors)
- Quality management systems (QMS) documentation and audit trails

## Key Activities
- Platform development and maintenance with regular security updates
- Regulatory compliance monitoring and framework updates
- Security audits and certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001, HITRUST)
- Customer integration support and custom development
- Continuous updates for changing medical device regulations globally

## Key Partners
- Medical device certification bodies (FDA notified bodies, CE marking agencies)
- Cloud infrastructure providers with healthcare compliance (AWS Healthcare, Google Cloud Healthcare)
- Hardware component manufacturers with medical device experience
- Regulatory consultants specializing in medical devices
- Medical research institutions and innovation centers

## Cost Structure
- Regulatory compliance and certification expenses: USD 200k-500k/year
- Security infrastructure and audits: USD 150k-300k/year
- Medical domain expert salaries: USD 300k-600k/year for team of 3-5
- Platform development and maintenance: USD 500k-1M/year
- Sales and marketing in specialized medical channels: USD 300k-700k/year
