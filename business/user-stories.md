# User Stories

## Epic 1: Hardware Integration

**As a** medical device engineer,
**I want** to easily integrate hardware components into the software platform,
**So that** I can streamline the development process and reduce time-to-market.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should support a variety of hardware interfaces (e.g., USB, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi).
  - The integration process should be documented and user-friendly.
  - The platform should provide real-time feedback during hardware integration.
  - The platform should support multiple hardware vendors.
  - The integration should be secure and compliant with medical-grade standards.

- **Complexity:** L

**As a** firmware developer,
**I want** to test firmware updates on the platform before deploying to actual devices,
**So that** I can ensure compatibility and functionality.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide a sandbox environment for firmware testing.
  - The platform should support rollback mechanisms in case of failed updates.
  - The platform should log all firmware update activities for traceability.
  - The platform should provide alerts for critical firmware issues.
  - The platform should support automated firmware update testing.

- **Complexity:** M

## Epic 2: Cloud Implementation

**As a** cloud architect,
**I want** to deploy medical-grade software to the cloud with minimal configuration,
**So that** I can ensure scalability and reliability.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should support major cloud providers (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
  - The platform should provide automated scaling options based on usage.
  - The platform should ensure data encryption and compliance with healthcare regulations.
  - The platform should provide monitoring and logging capabilities.
  - The platform should support high availability and disaster recovery.

- **Complexity:** L

**As a** data scientist,
**I want** to access and analyze medical data from the cloud securely,
**So that** I can derive insights and improve patient outcomes.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide secure data access mechanisms.
  - The platform should support data anonymization for privacy compliance.
  - The platform should provide tools for data visualization and analysis.
  - The platform should support integration with data science tools (e.g., Jupyter Notebooks).
  - The platform should provide audit trails for data access.

- **Complexity:** M

## Epic 3: Compliance and Security

**As a** compliance officer,
**I want** to ensure that the platform meets all medical-grade regulatory requirements,
**So that** we can avoid legal issues and ensure patient safety.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide documentation for regulatory compliance.
  - The platform should support regular security audits.
  - The platform should provide tools for compliance monitoring.
  - The platform should support automated compliance reporting.
  - The platform should provide training materials for compliance best practices.

- **Complexity:** L

**As a** security engineer,
**I want** to implement robust security measures to protect patient data,
**So that** we can prevent data breaches and ensure patient trust.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide encryption for data at rest and in transit.
  - The platform should support multi-factor authentication.
  - The platform should provide intrusion detection and prevention mechanisms.
  - The platform should support regular security updates.
  - The platform should provide tools for vulnerability assessment.

- **Complexity:** L

## Epic 4: User Experience

**As a** clinician,
**I want** to use the platform to access patient data and medical devices seamlessly,
**So that** I can provide better patient care.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide a user-friendly interface for clinicians.
  - The platform should support integration with electronic health records (EHRs).
  - The platform should provide real-time data from medical devices.
  - The platform should support customizable dashboards for different clinical roles.
  - The platform should provide training and support for clinicians.

- **Complexity:** M

**As a** patient,
**I want** to access my medical data and interact with medical devices securely,
**So that** I can manage my health more effectively.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide a secure patient portal.
  - The platform should support integration with wearable medical devices.
  - The platform should provide alerts and notifications for critical health data.
  - The platform should support data sharing with healthcare providers.
  - The platform should provide training and support for patients.

- **Complexity:** S

**As a** healthcare administrator,
**I want** to manage user access and permissions efficiently,
**So that** I can ensure data security and operational efficiency.

- **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - The platform should provide role-based access control (RBAC).
  - The platform should support integration with existing identity management systems.
  - The platform should provide tools for user provisioning and deprovisioning.
  - The platform should provide audit trails for user access.
  - The platform should provide training and support for administrators.

- **Complexity:** M
